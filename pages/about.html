About

“Weather On The Go” is not the average weather app. It provides the user with basic features such as the temperature, humidity, possibility of rain, wind speed, and the clarity of the sky. However, this app specializes in telling the user the weather based on their desired location or current location. Also, our app goes the extra mile to be interactive with the user by providing suggestions on what to wear and some activities to engage in. The goal is to provide a personalized experience when the user looks at the weather and plans their day.

The Purpose

The purpose of "Weather on The Go" is to provide a user experience unlike most weather apps. It is a user experience that is much more personal and prepares you for the weather. Our app is designed to give you suggestions such as "wearing a raincoat" or "you can take a walk at the park" in order to directly interact with the user. Additionally, we wanted to fill the void of curiosty. Some users may simply be curious about how other areas differ in terms of weather.

Instructions

Using "Weather On The Go" is a simple, straightforward process. The app first will provide weather information based on your current location using geolocation technology. Also, you can use the search bar on our "Home" page in order to learn about the weather in a different location from your own.

The Creation Process

"Weather on The Go" uses technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Also, we used GitHub for a collaboration tool. Lastly, we used the OpenWeatherMap API and the Unsplash Image API. These tools allow for the weather to be accessed, and allow images to be shown to the user which are related to the type of weather that is occurring.

Who Are We?

Hello! We are a group of students at Fisk University that were tasked to create a website of our choice. We started with ideas of creating a journaling system, a calender-like app, and a weather-based app. As a result, we were able to agree upon an app that would piece together all of our ideas. Therefore, we decided to create a weather app with a personalized touch to it. This is done through giving suggestions to the user such as what can be worn and what activites can be performed based on the weather.

The Future

The future of "Weather on The Go" involves possibly a dark mode, and there may be a future to log in your activities into a log or calendar based similar. This would be a daily system based on an account-like system. The user would be able to create an account for "Weather On The Go" which would then be used to keep a log of all your activities in a database.
